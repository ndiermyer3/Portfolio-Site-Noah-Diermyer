User Experience

Problems

“I would add some color to these activity options and make them larger. There’s a ton of white space and I think you could use it to make the screen look really fun and interactive.”

“I like when apps like this have apple or google sign in.”

“I want to be able to send reviews to my family.”

Solutions

Add color to all screens and pages to keep the users happy. Also add more to each page to take away too much white space.

Add Apple and Google pay for a fast and easy sighn up/sign in

Add the feature to send or recieve reviews

Conclusion

User friendly is key for the 5ive Star app.

After many hours of testing the users, we wanted an app that is easy to use and understand.

  • simple
  • see & write reviews

we want our users to be:

  • comfortable
  • happy
  • succcessful

In the future we want more users to read, write, and share reviews seemlessly in the comfort of their own phone.
